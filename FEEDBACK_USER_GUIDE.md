# User Feedback Form - User Guide

## How to Submit Feedback

### Step 1: Navigate to Feedback Page
Go to `/userfeedback` in your browser

### Step 2: Fill in Your Information
- **First Name** (required) - Enter your first name
- **Last Name** (required) - Enter your last name

### Step 3: Select Feedback Type
Choose one of the following:
- **Report Bugs** - Found something broken? Let us know
- **Request Features** - Have an idea for improvement?
- **General Feedback** - Share your thoughts
- **Other** - Any other feedback

### Step 4: Rate Your Experience (Optional)
Click on the stars to rate your overall experience:
- ‚≠ê 1 star - Poor
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê 2 stars - Fair
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê 3 stars - Good
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê 4 stars - Very Good
- ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê 5 stars - Excellent

**Tip:** Hover over stars to preview your rating before clicking

### Step 5: Add a Subject (Optional)
Provide a brief summary of your feedback (e.g., "Login page is slow")

### Step 6: Write Your Message (Required)
Write detailed information about your feedback:
- For bugs: Describe what happened and what you expected
- For features: Explain the feature and why it would help
- For general: Share your thoughts and suggestions

**Minimum:** 10 characters
**Maximum:** 5000 characters

### Step 7: Submit
Click the "Submit Feedback" button

### Step 8: Confirmation
You'll see a success message confirming your feedback was received

## What Happens After Submission

1. **Immediate Confirmation** - You see a success message
2. **Form Reset** - The form clears for new submissions
3. **Processing** - Our team reviews your feedback
4. **Response** - Admins may respond to your feedback

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to be logged in?
No! You can submit feedback without logging in. However, if you're logged in, we can track your feedback and respond to you directly.

### Will my feedback be private?
Your feedback is reviewed by our admin team. We may use it to improve the platform, but we won't share your personal information.

### How long does it take to get a response?
We review all feedback regularly. Response time depends on the type and complexity of your feedback.

### Can I edit my feedback after submitting?
Currently, you cannot edit feedback after submission. If you need to add more information, please submit additional feedback.

### What if I have an urgent issue?
For urgent technical issues, please contact our support team directly rather than using the feedback form.

## Troubleshooting

### Form Won't Submit
- Check that all required fields are filled
- Ensure your message is at least 10 characters
- Try refreshing the page
- Check your internet connection

### Validation Errors
- **First Name:** Must be filled in
- **Last Name:** Must be filled in
- **Feedback Type:** Must select a type
- **Message:** Must be 10-5000 characters

### Success Message Doesn't Appear
- Check browser console for errors (F12)
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache

### Still Having Issues?
Contact our support team for assistance
