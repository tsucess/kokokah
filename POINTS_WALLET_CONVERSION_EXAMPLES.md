# Points to Wallet Conversion - Practical Examples

## Conversion Ratio: 10 Points = ₦1.00

## Example 1: Student Completes Quiz

**Scenario**: Ahmed completes a quiz with 5 questions (20 points each)

### Initial State
- Points: 0
- Wallet: ₦0.00

### After Quiz Completion
- Questions answered correctly: 4/5 (80 points)
- Quiz pass bonus: +10 points
- **Total points earned: 90 points**
- Wallet: ₦0.00 (unchanged)

### After Conversion
- Converts: 90 points
- Calculation: 90 ÷ 10 = ₦9.00
- **New wallet balance: ₦9.00**
- **Remaining points: 0**

## Conversion Best Practices

1. **Convert in multiples of 10** for maximum efficiency
2. **Save points** if you're close to a course purchase
3. **Check wallet balance** before converting
4. **Track conversion history** for budgeting
5. **Plan conversions** around course releases
