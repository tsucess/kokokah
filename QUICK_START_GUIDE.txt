================================================================================

POST /api/lessons/{lessonId}/quizzes

Request:
{
  "title": "Question text",
  "type": "mcq",
  "questions": [{
    "question_text": "Question?",
    "type": "mcq",
    "options": ["Option 1", "Option 2"],
    "correct_answer": "Option 1",
    "points": 5
  }],
  "passing_score": 50,
  "shuffle_questions": false

{
  "success": true,
